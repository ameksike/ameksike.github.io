Services

Ksike Development Team (KSDT) is a collaborative group founded by Antonio Membrides Espinosa in 2010. KSDT promotes the development of computer applications. Among the main objectives are the exchange and learning of our members. As a result, we have various technologies, libraries, frameworks, demo applications, etc. Many of our products have been used in real projects for commercial purposes, although most of them are for academic purposes.

About

Below, we show some of the main companies in which this collaborative development group had direct or indirect involvement since its creation.

  • 2009-2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We started as graduates of the Informatics Science University (UCI) located in Havana, Cuba, carrying out projects oriented to the development of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and WEB applications fundamentally.

  • 2016-2018

    IT and Telecoms

    Execution of projects for the Desoft company, executing tasks related to server virtualization, installation and administration of network services (email, Jabber, LDAP, DHCP, DNS, FTP, HTTP, Proxy, Firewall, VPN, VLAN, Cloud, NAS), creation of the institutional corporate portal, as well as tools for the administration and control of services.

  • February 2019

    Service and Develop

    Execution of projects for the Labiofam company aimed at the development of corporate intranet, directories and search systems, business management applications, administration of network services, etc.

  • September 2019

    Research and Development

    Executing projects oriented to the development and use of the latest technological trends, as well as the linking of different technologies and methodologies to obtain robust products. Excited about challenging enterprise environments, technological innovations, open-source platforms and future trends.
